Vision
Our community aims to paint the moon. The moon has been a landmark for all living things on our earth since the existence of mankind. He can be seen in different forms: Sometimes small and sometimes large.
As the world population continues to grow, we are forced to colonize the moon in the near future. The effort for this will be enormous. In the future, buildings, green areas and rivers will be created on the moon.
Our project aims to make our moon not ugly. We want to draw attention to it early enough not to deface our moon. We want to create the moon in its most beautiful form. It should appear beautiful and not ugly when seen from our earth. We do not want an ugly moon. We want a beautiful landmark that continues to exist as a point of light for humanity and our wildlife.
We are UGLY Coin.
